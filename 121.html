Solution · Two Pointers / Sliding Window
1
Init: left = 0 (buy day), maxProfit = 0. right iterates every day as a potential sell day.
2
Found cheaper buy? If prices[left] > prices[right], move left = right and continue — no point selling at a loss.
3
Update profit: otherwise compute prices[right] − prices[left] and update maxProfit if it's larger.
leftrightactionmaxProfit
0(7)1(1)7>1 → left=10
1(1)2(5)5−1=44
1(1)3(3)3−1=24
1(1)4(6)6−1=55 ✓
1(1)5(4)4−1=35
Key rule: we never sell on a day cheaper than our buy day. Moving left to the new minimum guarantees we always have the best possible buy price as we scan forward.
