Find Median from Data Stream

Continuously add numbers and return the median in O(log n).

MaxHeap (left): [5,4,3]
MinHeap (right): [6,7,8]

Balanced heaps → median is either top of left OR average of both tops

Always keep left maxHeap ≥ right minHeap size
1️⃣ Insert into left (maxHeap)
2️⃣ Move largest from left → right (minHeap)
3️⃣ Rebalance if right.size > left.size
4️⃣ Median:
• If uneven → left.peek()
• If equal → average of both
💡 Why?
Left holds smaller half, right holds larger half
Time: O(log n)
Space: O(n)
