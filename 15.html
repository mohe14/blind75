Solution · Sort + Fix + Two Pointers
1
Sort nums. This enables two-pointer logic and makes duplicate detection trivial.
2
Fix i in outer loop. Skip if i > 0 && nums[i] == nums[i-1] to avoid duplicate triplets.
3
Two pointers left = i+1, right = n-1. Move based on sum vs. zero.

Skip inner duplicates on match: advance left while nums[left] == nums[left+1], and right while nums[right] == nums[right-1], then move both.
3 duplicate checks total: outer loop skip on i, inner skip on left, inner skip on right — all after a match is found.
